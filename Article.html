Article

Def:

Article are the words which make the thing definite or indefinite.

TYPES

There are two types of article:

1.Definite article

2.Indefinite article

1.Definite article:

The definite article (the) is used before a noun to indicate that the identity of the noun is known to the reader.

The are definite article.

2.Indefinite article.

The indefinite article (a, an) is used before a noun that is general or when its identity is not known.

An,a is a definite article.

USE OF INDEFINITE ARTICLE:

An is use before those noun which start from vowels or vowels sound.

Such as;

An apple,An orange etc.

A is use before those noun which does not start from vowels or does not give vowels sound.

Such as;

A book,A car etc.
